SEW - Software Engeneering Week

CESIUM- University of Minho


The Software Engineering Week is an event organized by Cesium, which brings together several companies in one space for a week.

In addition to all companies having contact sites with students, there are several lectures and workshops on topics related to information technology.

I like to participate in the most enriching way, not only by attending the lectures but also by speaking with the companies present to try to understand what is the present and what will be the future.


I also like to participate in the Journeys of Informatic (JOIN) which is an event organized by Cesium and NECC which is very similar to SEW.


World Scouting Congess (WSC)

QUEST - Sports Solutions


WSC is a congress that has as main objective the sharing of knowledge in the scouting area in the world of football.

It's an event that brings together several people from the football world, such as coaches, scouts, players, etc. to address the future of the sport.

In the year that I participated, there was a very interesting panel for me, which addressed the topic "IT in a more effective scouting".


Challanje

Base - IPAM & ANJE


Challanje was a junior creativity marathon, organized by Base -IPAM and ANJE, where the objective was to develop a pitch where we present our response to real challenges presented by companies.

They arranged groups of five people and assigned a problem / challenge to each group. We had 12 hours to find solutions to the challenge and at the end of the day we had to make a presentation of a maximum of 5 minutes with our solution.
