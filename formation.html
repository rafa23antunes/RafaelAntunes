Degree in Computer Science

Digital Marketing Course

AAUM (Students Association of the University of Minho) [Nov, 2017 - 30 hours]


It is part of a range of courses organized by the student association of the University of Minho every year. It is an initiative with the name, in English, "complement yourself" in which students can, only if they want, enroll and take a short course to complement their degree.

It was in this course that I had my first contact with HTML & CSS, but I also learned about several other tools such as Google AdSense, AdWords, MailChimp, and other marketing concepts such as the notion of niches, target audience, perfect timming ...

English Conversation Course B2

BabeliUM, Language Center - University of Minho [Jun/Jul, 2019 - 48 hours]


I entered this course with the purpose of improving my speech in English.

Presential classes with an excellent teacher who helped us all to improve vocabulary, but mainly to speak and communicate in a more fluid way.

We also evaluated other aspects besides speech. I ended with a B2 assessment in writing and reading. In oral production, the teacher rated me with B2+.
